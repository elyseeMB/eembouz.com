Durand Construction

Development of the showcase website for Durand Construction, a Brazzaville-based company specializing in construction and architecture. The site highlights their projects, services, and technical approach through a clear and structured layout.

Overview

Durand Construction is a company based in Brazzaville, active in the fields of building construction and engineering.
The showcase website was designed to present their projects, services, and work philosophy in a clear and functional way.

Design

The interface is built around a modular layout, centered on a clean typography system and a visual rhythm inspired by architectural design principles. The structure highlights the key sections — Work, About, and Blog — with a readable and balanced hierarchy.

The website was developed using a static architecture, favoring speed, security, and minimal maintenance.

Content

The site showcases a selection of recent projects, the services offered, and a blog area dedicated to sharing articles about architecture, sustainability, and educational insights related to the profession.

The overall goal is to strengthen the company’s online presence and highlight their expertise through clean and refined communication.

Technologies

The project relies on several complementary technologies: Astro for static site generation, ensuring speed and performance; TypeScript for more reliable and maintainable code; CSS for visual structure and styling; and Markdown for simple and efficient content management.
